About me

Hello, I am Michael Daryl P. Mayo. This is my personal portfolio I created for Free Code Camp. I am currently studying BS Computer Engineering at Technological Institute of the Philippines. I am already in my second year now.

Since I was a child, I have always been fascinated by computers or technology. I am curious on how things work and how they were made. I still remember disassembling my toys to see how those components inside them interacts to make them work. That is the reason why I enter the field of technology.

I took up my kindergarten education in Bicol, Philippines. Preparatory to Elementary and Elementary in Victoria School Foundation in Manila. When I was in grade 3 I transferred to Concepcion Elementary School and graduated ranked 6. I then enrolled in Marikina Science High School and passed. I finished high school with the award "Programmer and Technician of the Year" (I'm really proud of it haha) and found my way unto college.

I joined a lot of competitions, including Tagisang Robotics. Tagisang Robotics is a competition held by the Department of Science and Technology wherein robots are designed by each of the schools who attended. The robots will then compete in a soccer match. I really enjoy and value this experience so much because it is one of the reasons why am I pursuing my degree right now. It is one of the lights I keep holding on whenever I feel sad and hopeless.

I love reading novels (fiction and non-fiction, more on the genre romance), reading books, watching TV series like How I Met Your Mother, The Big Bang Theory and many more. I spend my time on surfing the web specifically the website Reddit, learning programming through Harvard CS50x online, and reading a lot of translated foreign web novels. Right now I am currently studying web development through Free Code Camp and I am aiming to take the Full-Stack Developer Certification.

In the near future, I plan to widen my knowledge in web development and finish CS50. After that, I plan to study Network Security. By the end of 2018, I hope that I can get an Intern position in a good company.
